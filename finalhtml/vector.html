I wanted to make this project a little humurous. I decided to make a snake in the desert with cacti around. I was inspired by the snake from Robin Hood, Sir Hiss. To make it more playful I made toxic waste and made it look like the snake thought it was water. I put an outter glow to show the aftermath of consuming the toxins.
