RASTER PROJECT

The concept that I was going for was peace and gazing into the universe. I incorporated daisies as stars. The quote was to match with not letting anything around disturb your inner peace.

It is always good to take a step back to collect yourself and move forward.

ILLUSTRATOR PROJECT

I wanted to make this project a little humurous. I decided to make a snake in the desert with cacti around. I was inspired by the snake from Robin Hood, Sir Hiss. To make it more playful I made toxic waste and made it look like the snake thought it was water. I put an outter glow to show the aftermath of consuming the toxins.

ANIMATION PROJECT

For my animation project, I decided to create a hummingbird roaming through the flowers. I threw in some wind, tulips and a lady bug crawling up the side. I wanted the hummingbird to be colorful and make it during the spring season.
